ABOUT THE MAP
  • All statistics gathered from this site
  • Data parsed and overlaid onto a map of the world
  • Colors are automatically assigned to a country based on its numerical value for that dataset & year
  • Intended to be used as a comparative tool for worldwide datasets between countries.
  • One tab: Choose one dataset and a year, and that year's data will be overlaid onto the map.
  • Ratio tab: Choose one dataset and two years, and the difference in values as a percentage of the original will be overlaid onto the map.
  • Graph tab: Choose two datasets, two years, and which graphs you want to view, then hit submit, and enjoy.
